I am a PhD student in the <a href="https://atlarge-research.com/">Massivizing Computer Systems research group</a>, part of the <a href="https://www.vucompsys.net/">Computer Systems Section</a> of the <a href="https://vu.nl/en/about-vu/faculties/faculty-of-science/departments/computer-science">Computer Science Department</a> at <a href="https://vu.nl/en">Vrije Universiteit Amsterdam</a>.

I do research on the compute continuum, or how user devices such as laptops and smartphones interact with cloud datacenters and vice versa.
In this continuum, users may play online games that require a connection to the cloud, or watch movies that need to be streamed from the cloud.
My active interest concerns analyzing, understanding, and optimizing the computer systems that make this compute continuum possible.
